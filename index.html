Short tale abt my life

  • I was born in Krasnohorivka city, Ukraine on July 15, 2003. There I went in the kindergarten and my first school.

  • I had carefree childhood here for 10 years until war had knocked in my door. Russia-paid terrorists and separatistes started bombing and occupying cities so my parents decided to move. I hoped this war would not last long, but it`s already 8 years of living away from home. Before we finally moved to Kamianske, we changed 3 towns. It made me change 3 schools and this is the reason why I lost 6th educational year.

  • Last 4 educational years I spent there. I never liked any school subjects, except history, geography and languages. Mainly it`s because of teachers.So I had graduated school with the middle marks, but I passed EIE (Ukrainian final school exam) very well. I also took 2nd place in the all-Ukraian weightlifting championship once. I had understood I don`t like a lot of jobs, but I really love languages, history and literature so i decided to study as an interpreter. I went to the Oles Honchar Dnipro National University in 2020. Covid-19 made us studying distantly, so last two years I spent mostly at home.

  • War, schools and cities changing, lockdown, misunderstandings made me closed. I went from an extrovert to an introvert and developed social anxiety.
